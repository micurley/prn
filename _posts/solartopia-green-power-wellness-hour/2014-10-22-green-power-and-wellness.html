 Linda has worked in San Luis Obispo, California, for more than thirty years to close the two huge reactors at DIABLO CANYON, half way between Los Angeles and San Francisco.

But now their owner, Pacific Gas & Electric, is uniquely vulnerable.
 A powerful grassroots upsurge is moving fast forward to shut them down.
