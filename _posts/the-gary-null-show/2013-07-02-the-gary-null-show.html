From 1987 to 1992 he was a researcher at Stanford University School of Medicine.
Among his discoveries was identifying a mechanism in cells that function like a brain and the founding of a field of cellular biology known as epigenetics.
Dr Lipton is now recognized as one of the leading voices in the New Biology and Mind-Body Medicine.
