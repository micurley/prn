Dr CE Gant has practiced integrative and functional medicine and more recently genomics for over 35 years.
He is best known for his work in the treatment of mental and substance use disorders.
He received his medical degree from the University of Virginia Medical School, and his PhD in psychology from Columbia Pacific University.
For two decades, Dr Gant pioneered many of the nutritional, metabolic, neurotransmitter and hormonal balancing, and detoxification treatments for substance use and other mental disorders while serving as the medical director of Tully Hill Hospital, as medical consultant at Syracuse Behavioral Healthcare and as a psychiatric consultant at many substance abuse and mental health, clinics and hospitals, throughout Central New York.
Dr Gant currently practices functional medicine, genomics and mindfulness-based psychotherapy at the National Integrated Health Associates in Washington, DC and teaches these cutting-edge, science-based, methodologies at the Academy of Functional Medicine, Dentistry and Psychology.
