And the launch of her new radio program on PRN Louise Habakus is the co-founder and Director of the Center  for Personal Rights – a non profile advocacy organization dedicated to healthy parenting, education, and the protection of vaccination choice as an essential human and civil right.
She is the co-founder along with Alison McNeil, daughter of Robert McNeil from PBS’s McNeil-Leher, and others of Nurture Parenting and Fearless Parent, a grassroots community and online media organization to educate parents about holistic parenting for raising healthy children.
 The website is Fearless-Parent.com   Louise has an MBA from Sanford University and is the co-author “Vaccine Epidemic: How Corporate Greed, Biased Science and Coercive Government Threaten Our Human Rights, Our Health and Our Children”   Her new radio broadcast – Fearless Parent Radio – will air every Wednesday at 11 am Eastern Time over the Progressive Radio Network starting tomorrow.
