Dr Omar Manejwala is a nationally recognized expert in addiction, addiction psychiatry and the psychological phenomenon of “craving” who integrates neurobiological sciences with integrative behavior practice, such as somatic therapy, to promote transformative change in patients.
  He is currently the Medical Director of the Hazeldon Foundation that specializes the study and treatment of addiction behavior.
Dr Manejwala received his medical degree from the University of Maryland School of Medicine and was an Executive Chief Resident of Psychiatry at Duke University.
He is the author of the recently released book, “Craving: Why We Cant Seem to Get Enough.”
