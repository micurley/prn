 Dr Davidson’s most recent publication – “After the Sheikhs: The Coming Collapse of the Gulf Monarchies” predicts that the pieces are in place for the downfall of the autocratic ruling families across the Gulf States during the next 2 to 5 years.
The book has been reviewed as among the Most Significant Books of 2013 by the Foreign Policy Association and among the top ten best books on the Middle East by Foreign Policy journal and the Al-Monitor.
 The Economist recognizes him as one of the most knowledgeable academics writing about the Gulf region.
Christopher has a BA and Masters from the University of Cambridge and received an additional masters and a doctorate from the University of St.
