Dr. Paul Craig Roberts is one of today’s more progressive analytical and economic writers and commentators on the state of the nation: governmental dysfunction, political corruption, global and domestic economic threats and decay, our wars on terror and our Middle East conflicts, and the decline in human values in American society.
He is the chairman of the Institute for Political Economy is the former Assistant Secretary of Treasury during Ronald Reagan’s first term.
During his academic career he taught at many universities and held the William Simon Chair in Political Economy at the Center for Strategic and International Studies at Georgetown University.
Dr Roberts is a frequently contributor to news and commentary sites such as Global Research, Counterpunch, Creators Syndicate, Opednews, and LewRockwell.
