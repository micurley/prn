He is currently the Senior Fellow at the Post Carbon Institute in Northern California.
 He was featured in Leonardo DiCaprio’s film “The Eleventh Hour” and frequently appears on major media networks to address our fossil fuel based culture and the future of climate change.
Richard is the author of many books, including “The Party’s Over” which has become  a standard text for the peak oil position.
