Dr. Raphael Kellman, MD is an internal medical physician in private practice in mid-town Manhattan who has been practicing alternative and complementary medicine for almost 20 years.
  He was a founder of Mind-Body New York, one of the country’s most advanced integrative health centers, which offered pioneering and innovative treatments for many diseases  including cancer, heart disease, chronic fatigue syndrome, fibromyalgia and autoimmune conditions Dr Kellman integrates the best of scientifically proven therapeutic and prevention modalities from both conventional and natural health systems.
