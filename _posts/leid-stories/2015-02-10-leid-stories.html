(Check the archives at leidstories.podbean.

) Dr Lumpkins is a lecturer of labor and employment relations at Pennsylvania State University, where he earned both his doctorate in history in 2006, and teaches history and African American studies.
He also holds a master’s degree in library science.
His scholastic research focuses particularly on the history of social and political movements, and the history of the working-class.
Dr Lumpkins is the author of the highly praised American Pogrom: The East St.
Louis Race Riot and Black Politics, a necessary historical reference for the state of affairs in Ferguson, Missouri, today.
