” The triangulation of good ol’ American exceptionalism in Washington — the executive, legislative and judicial branches of government — has killed it.
Dead.
Democracy is out.
Kakistocracy — government by the most unprincipled people, the worst elements in our society — is in.
The current situation is a case study in the dramatic shift that has occurred at practically all levels of government, creating a relationship between those who govern and those governed that more closely resembles that between predator and prey.
It’s not government by the people, for the people; it’s government despite the people, intentionally insulated against their will.
Leid Stories explains how we got to this point and what can be done about it.
