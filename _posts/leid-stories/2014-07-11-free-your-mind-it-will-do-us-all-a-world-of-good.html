It Will Do Us All A World of Good! --- Just for you, it’s “Free Your Mind Friday” on Leid Stories! It’s when listeners flip the script on major news issues and events, or on any subject they choose.
There’s only one requirement for participating in this free-wheeling discussion: You must have an opinion and be able to defend it; neutrality is a total copout.
Bring your considered opinions and ideas to the best open forum and test your—and our—intellectual mettle.
We are nice people with good manners.
We will receive your offering with great respect.
Join us.
Free your mind.
It will do us all a world of good!
