So much has happened where you live, across the nation and around the world, it’s enough to make you lose your mind.
But don’t.
It’s “Free Your Mind Friday” on Leid Stories—the perfect antidote to mental meltdown.
Let’s hear your thoughts and opinions on issues and events that mattered this week, or on any other subject you think warrants special attention.
Here there’s no “right” or “wrong” way of seeing the world; we’re interested in the logic and integrity of your position and, truth be told, a sense of humor while we banter.
The main task, you see, is to vent with purpose.
This way, we declutter, recalibrate and ready ourselves for a great weekend with family and friends.
