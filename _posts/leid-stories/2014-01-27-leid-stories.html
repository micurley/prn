Obama’s SOTU 2014, therefore, will reflect the chastened president’s new reality: launching any major legislative offensives this year and leading up to 2016 is politically too risky, both for his legacy and the fortunes of the Democratic Party.
Obama will pursue an agenda that is expedient and safe.
The hurting constituencies that elected him should hold out no hopes that their needs top his list.
