They’re not in a hurry; in fact, they really, really want to take their time—say, another three years or so.
Good government is not to be rushed!
The Republicans are relishing a plethora of opportunities to score points with jaded voters and regroup their fractured party.

Leid Stories explains why this “perfect storm” of political liabilities, though designed to sink Obama, could be made to backfire.
