It airs on the progressiveradionetwork.com out of New York City title: Project Censored tags: - News & Politics - project-censored title: 4th of July Special show on American Free Thought --- For the July 4 Project Censored Show on Pacifica Radio,  Mickey Huff and  Peter Phillips pre-recorded a special program... in honor of the 4th of July and American Independence.
They examine the history of American Free Thought and the importance of Freedom of Inquiry to American Society.
  Their guests include: Roderick Bradford producer of the four-hour documentary American Freethought, covering the history of censorship and secularism in the US.
Joining them also is Tom Flynn executive director of the Council of Secular Humanism, and editor of Free Inquiry magazine.
