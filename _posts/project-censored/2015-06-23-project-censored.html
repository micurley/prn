It airs on the progressiveradionetwork.com out of New York City title: Project Censored tags: - News & Politics - project-censored title: Project Censored --- Following a nonfatal outbreak of measles that began at Disneyland, the California Legislature is debating a bill (SB... 277) to outlaw parental choice about childhood vaccinations.
The bill would include the measles vaccine, as well as nine others.
  Today’s guests support vaccinations, but oppose making them compulsory; they discuss the issues involved from a variety of perspectives, including law, civil rights, and medical ethics.
Caitlyn Obolsky and Robert Krakow are attorneys; Elliot Cohen teaches ethics at Florida State University Medical School; Sharyl Attkisson is an investigative journalist; Lori Martin Gregory is editor of the Mom Street Journal.
