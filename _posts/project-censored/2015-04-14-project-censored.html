It airs on the progressiveradionetwork.com out of New York City title: Project Censored tags: - News & Politics - project-censored title: Project Censored --- This week’s Project Censored Show focuses on police violence and prisons. The program begins with Carl Dix examining the fatal shooting of Walter Scott in South Carolina.
Then Jeff Mackler gives an update on the medical condition of Pennsylvania inmate Mumia abu Jamal, and we hear abu Jamal’s latest recorded commentary.
In the second half of the program, Maya Schenwar discusses her new book about the prison system, “Locked Down, Locked Out,” and makes the case for shrinking the prison system.Carl Dix is cofounder of the Stop-Mass-Incarceration Network; Jeff Mackler is director of the Mobilization to Free Mumia abu Jamal. Maya Schenwar is editor-in-chief of Truthout.
