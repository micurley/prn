Grote explains that Abu-Jamal was allowed to develop diabetes without treatment to the point that he lost consciousness, and was then hospitalized with relatives and even his lawyers barred from visiting him or learning his condition.
The lawsuit demands access to Abu-Jamal and to his medical records, and access to him by physicians obtained by his family.
