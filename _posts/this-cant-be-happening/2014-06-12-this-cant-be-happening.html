Grant says Bergdahl clearly was a young man questioning the war he found himself in, posted in a lonely, dangerous and remote mountain area much like where he grew up in his native Idaho.
He says the case is a war of narratives, with conservative ‘chickenhawks’ in Congress and the media (and a ’senile’ Sen.
