Dr. Arem is an endocrinologist and Director of the Texas Thyroid Institute in Houston, Texas.
Dr Arem trained in Paris at CochinHospital and College de France with Professor Raymond Michel, a researcher involved in the discovery of T3, the active form of thyroid hormone.
He also studied at SouthBaltimoreGeneralHospital and JohnsHopkinsHospital, and completed his fellowship in endocrinology at Baylor College of Medicine, where he is a clinical professor of medicine.
He is a fellow of the AmericanCollege of Physicians, fellow of the AmericanCollege of Endocrinology, and a long standing member of the American Thyroid Association.
He is board certified in Internal Medicine and Endocrinology and Metabolism.
He served as chief of endocrinology and metabolism at BenTaub GeneralHospital in Houston for many years and is the author of the ground-breaking best-seller The Thyroid Solution.
