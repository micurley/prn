Warshowsky, OD, FAAO, FCOVD. Dr. Warshowsky is a holistic behavioral and developmental Optometrist.
He is Associate Clinical Professor Emeritus and founding Chief of Pediatrics at SUNY State College of Optometry, where he taught for 37 years.
 He is author of the book “HOW BEHAIORAL OPTOMETRY CAN UNLOCK YOUR CHILD’S POTENTIAL,” published by Jessica Kingsley Publishers, London and USA.
  He has served as Optometric Consultant to numerous schools for child development throughout New York and New Jersey.
Dr Warshowsky has lectured internationally and is published widely in the field of optometry, and is a Fellow of the AmericanAcademy of Optometry and College of Optometrists in Vision Development.
He maintains two holistic pediatric and vision therapy practices in New York and New Jersey.
