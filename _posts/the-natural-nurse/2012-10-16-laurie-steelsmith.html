Dr Steelsmith is a licensed naturopathic physician and acupuncturist with a 20-year private practice in Honolulu.
At BastyrUniversity, Dr Steelsmith  earned a Masters degree in acupuncture.
She studied both traditional Chinese medicine and Five-element acupuncture, as well as Chinese herbal medicine and nutrition.
She is licensed by the state of Hawaii as both a naturopathic physician and an acupuncturist.
In addition to the degrees she earned from BastyrUniversity, Dr Steelsmith practiced as a licensed massage therapist in Washington state for eight years and was also an American Council on Exercise certified excerise instructor and aerobics teacher.
A leading spokesperson on natural medicine, she has appeared widely on TV, radio, and in print.
She and her husband, Alex Steelsmith, are coauthors of Great Sex, Naturally: Every Woman’s Guide to Enhancing Her Sexuality Through the Secrets of Natural Medicine (Hay House, 2012).
