   Processed food represents an entirely new way of eating for human beings.
Our diets have changed more in the last 100 years than they have in the last 10,000, and so much of what we now eat is deeply out of synch with our biology.
While the enormous amounts of technology applied to food over the last century have been a boon to manufacturing efficiency, product innovation and lowered food costs, most of it has been disastrous for our health.
