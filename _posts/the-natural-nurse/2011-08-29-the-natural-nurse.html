Dr Melillo is an adjunct professor of functional neuroanatomy and associate professor of clinical neurology and childhood behavior disorders.
He is an internationally known author, lecturer and researcher within the field of neurology, specializing in childhood disorders.
His personal mission to find a cure for neurobehavioral disorders began after helping a child achieve life-altering behavioral improvements.
He has since devoted himself to the study and treatment of learning disabilities and behavioral disorders in children, such as Autism, ADHD, and Asperger’s.
Dr Melillo is the originator of the Brain Balance Program®  and author of the bestselling book Reconnected Kids.
His ground breaking approach helps children with neuro-behavioral deficits improve their academic, social and behavioral functions using a drug-free method.
