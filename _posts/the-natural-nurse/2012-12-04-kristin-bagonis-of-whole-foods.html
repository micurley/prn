Kristin is the Regional marketing program manager for Whole Foods Market in Florida.
Before moving to Florida over eight years ago, Kristin grew up in Bucks County, Pennsylvania and graduated from the University of Pittsburgh with a Degree in Business Marketing.
 During the years she has been with Whole Foods, she served as the Marketing Team Leader handling events, community partnerships, and donations, before advancing to Regional Program Manager.
Kristin has always had an interest in travel and volunteerism which led her on a recent trip to Africa through Whole Foods Market’s Team Member Volunteer Program.
On todays show, Kristin will share information about Whole Foods Market’s Team Member Volunteer Program, Whole Planet Foundation, and Comfort the Children International, which she was involved with as part of a community development project in Kenya.
She is personally committed to how each individual can positively influence the world, on a personal, local and global level.
